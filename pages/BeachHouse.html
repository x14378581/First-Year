Beach House:

Key points about this Beach House:

This beach house is situated in Ireland, in the county of Wexford.
Wexford is known for its tourist attractions as it is family orientated.
There's the famous cove which consists of a restaurant, bowling alley,
minnie golf and a kids jungle gym area. Wexford also offers forest walks
and an adventure park named Gravity Extreme Adventure which consists of
zip wires, climbing wall, aerial treking and a FAN jump.
These attraction is within a 2 miles of the beach house.

    The beach house consists of :
  1. 4 bedrooms and can sleep 8 people, 1 king bed in master, 3 double beds
  2. Recently updated modern kitchen
  3. 3 bathrooms, 1 in the master bedroom, 1 on the top floor/bottom floor
  4. Beach on the front step
  5. Pets are welcome
  6. Walking distance from most attractions
  7. Parking Space
